Scrambled Egg Whites

My all time favorite breakfast is scrambled egg whites. Between 8AM and 9 AM I make this delectaable treat. Why not make scrambled eggs? My body doesn't like the yoke. So, scarmbled egg whites is my breakfast of choice because I don't like yoke and it is filling.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup of Spinach
  • 1/4 Cup of Tomatoes
  • 1/4 Cup of Mushrooms
  • 1/8 Cup of Cheese
  • 1 Teaspoon of Butter

What to Do!

  1. Heat the butter in a skillet on medium heat.
  2. Add the tomatoes, mushrooms and spinach to the skillet.
  3. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites.
  4. Add the egg whites to the skillet.
  5. Wait until the egg whites have firmed up, then scramble the egg whites.
  6. Enjoy!
